# Batty

![Batty icon](Icon.png)

A menu bar app that shows you where your MacBook's power is actually going — and helps you squeeze more life out of your battery.

## What is this?

Batty brings an energy flow screen to your Mac. Watch power flow between your charger, battery, and system in real time.

**Know exactly what's happening:**
- How many watts you're pulling from the wall right now
- How much power is flowing into (or out of) your battery
- How your screen brightness, CPU, GPU, and other components are draining your battery — updated live as you work

**Make smarter choices:**

Dimming your screen from 100% to 50% saves ~6W. Is that extra brightness worth 30 minutes of battery? Now you can see the tradeoff in real time and decide for yourself.

## Why?

macOS estimates update slowly and don't reflect what you're doing *right now*. Batty uses a calibrated power model that responds instantly to changes in brightness, workload, and power state — so you always know where you stand.

## Install

Download the latest release and drag to Applications. Batty lives in your menu bar and does not have a dock icon. Updates are delivered automatically via Sparkle.
